Ingredients

  • Rice

Rice

Description

A swamp grass that is widely cultivated as a source of food, especially in Asia.

Required Stuff

  • Pot
  • Stove

Directions
  1. Place however much rice you want to your pot
  2. Add water to the pot until it covers the rice plus a little bit or until it looks kinda like enough water
  3. Cook on high or some other temp until the water gets hot
  4. Wait 2-100 minutes or until rice looks done
